Short Bio

I have a track record of developing data-driven solutions for products in various domains. My current passion is to leverage AI for enhancing security. Prior to joining Microsoft, I was a Senior Technical Staff Member at IBM T.J. Watson Research Center and received the “Master Inventor” award from IBM in 2020. I also taught courses in NYU and Columbia universities. You can follow me on LinkedIn and/or Twitter to know more about me.
