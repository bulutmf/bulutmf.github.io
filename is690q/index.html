Course Description

As a result of increasing use of devices that have access to the Internet, we have witnessing an increasing amount of data that we haven't seen before. We have generated the 90% of world's data only in the last couple years and trends indicate that it will grow even futher.

Companies and organizations have already been started to embrace the "Big Data", simply because its potential to understand the reality and predict the future better. However, one must consider the different aspects of dealing with Big Data including storage and analytics.

In this course, we will learn the importance of embracing "Big Data" from an IT perspective. We will answer the questions of what are the main drivers of "Big Data" phenemonen in addition to how to store and process a large amount of data. We will also get our hands dirty and do programming exercises to obtain hands on experience on processing and analyzing Big Data.
